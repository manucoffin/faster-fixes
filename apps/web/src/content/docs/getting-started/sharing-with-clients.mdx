title: Sharing with Clients
description: Create reviewer tokens and share review links with your clients.
---

## What are reviewers?

Reviewers are the people who leave feedback — typically non-technical clients or stakeholders. They don't need a Faster Fixes account. Access is controlled through tokens.

## Creating a reviewer

In your project dashboard, go to **Reviewers** → **Create Reviewer**. Give each reviewer a descriptive name (e.g. "Sarah - Product Manager"). A unique token is generated automatically.

## Sharing the review link

The review link appends `?ff_token=<token>` to your website URL:

```
https://myapp.com?ff_token=abc123def456
```

Share this link with your client. When they open it, the widget activates and their feedback is attributed to them.

## Token persistence

Once a reviewer opens the link, the token is stored in their browser's `localStorage`. They can revisit the site without needing the token URL again.

## Managing reviewers

| Action | Effect |
| --- | --- |
| **Revoke** | Temporarily disable a reviewer's access. Their existing feedback remains visible. |
| **Restore** | Re-enable a previously revoked reviewer. |
| **Delete** | Permanently remove a reviewer and disassociate their feedback. |

## Best practices

- **One token per person** — Create a separate reviewer for each person so feedback is clearly attributed.
- **Use descriptive names** — Names like "Sarah - Product Manager" make it easy to identify who left each piece of feedback.
- **Revoke after review** — Disable tokens once the review phase is complete to prevent stale feedback.
